Complete Guide to eKYC Ladki Bahin Yojana Maharashtra

The Government of Maharashtra has introduced a revolutionary step for women empowerment. To receive financial assistance, completing the ladki bahin yojana ekyc is mandatory. This process ensures that the funds are transferred directly to the authenticated bank accounts of the beneficiaries.

How to Complete Ladki Bahin Maharashtra Gov in Login KYC?

Many users search for the exact steps to fulfill their ekyc ladki bahin requirement. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit the official portal. (See official link below)
  2. Navigate to the Ladki Bahin Maharashtra Gov in Login KYC section.
  3. Enter your registered mobile number and verify it via OTP.
  4. Enter your Aadhaar details to initiate the mukhyamantri ladki bahin yojana ekyc.
  5. Submit your biometric or OTP verification.

Whether you refer to it as eKYC or spell it phonetically as Ladki bahin kevaaysee, the digital authentication process remains exactly the same.

Special Categories: Vidhva Ladki Bahin eKYC

Widows in the state are given special priority. The Vidhva Ladki Bahin eKYC process is simplified to ensure fast-track approvals. Widows need to submit their husband's death certificate along with standard documents to get their names approved on the Ladki Bahin Maharashtra gov in list.

Important Deadlines

Beneficiaries are highly encouraged not to wait until the Ladki bahin ekyc last date 2026. Completing your authentication early ensures that your monthly benefits continue without any pause or suspension.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana Official Website? +
The official website of Majhi Ladki Bahin Maharashtra Yojana is https://ladakibahin.maharashtra.gov.in/. Always use this official link for registration and updates.
Is the Ladki Bahin e-KYC in English available? +
Yes, the portal supports multiple languages. You can switch the language settings on the website to complete your Ladki Bahin e-KYC in English or Marathi based on your comfort.
How can I check my name in the Ladki Bahin Maharashtra gov in list? +
After you successfully complete the ladki bahin ekyc, your application will be verified. You can log into your account dashboard on the official portal to view the approved beneficiary list.
